Paper Submission Guidelines

We welcome high-quality articles reporting novel and unpublished results of conceptual, creative, experimental, and theoretical work or study in any of the topic areas mentioned in Call For Papers. When a manuscript is submitted, it indicates that it is not being considered for publication elsewhere.

Send full manuscript via Email : csconference@psgcas.ac.in

Language:

The text should be in English, wherein the author should not mix both American and British usage.

Authors are instructed to proofread the article before submission in order to eliminate possible grammatical or spelling errors and to ensure the usage of correct scientific English.

Page Limit:

The maximum page count is 10, and the minimum page count is 3.

Submission Method:

Papers should only be submitted to the conference Email. The Email subject should clearly include the TRACK [The Email subject line should be “New paper submission to Track-1 / Track-2 ”] to which the author is submitting the paper.

Paper Structure:

The full paper should contain the following details of the proposed research work:

  • Title of the Paper
  • Abstract
  • Key Words (Not more than Five Key Words)
  • Design/ Methodology/ Approach
  • Purpose
  • Major Research Findings
  • Practical Implications
  • Research Limitations/ Implications
  • Originality/Value
  • Conclusion & Future Research Work

All submissions must be written in English, either in an MS Word format and must use standard manuscript template that can be downloaded below.

Paper Template: Word Template will be Given.

Review Process:

  • All articles will be peer-reviewed in a double-blind manner. Relevancy, structure, research technique, ethical conduct, language standard, inventiveness, references, hypothesis, result presentation, proficiency, format, and a variety of other aspects are all considered throughout the process of assessment.
  • Papers that are accepted will be published in the Conference Proceedings and further it will be published in the author selected publication track.
  • All submitted papers will be reviewed by three international program committees. All submissions will be screened for plagiarism and similarity check using Turnitin Software. By submitting your work, you agree to allow the conference team to screen your work for plagiarism.
  • The paper will be evaluated based on their novelty and innovative research contribution.

Decision:

There are three possible decisions for a submitted full-paper

  • Accepted with Official Acceptance mail to proceed with the registration.
  • Major Revision, the papers should be revised according to reviewer comments to obtain the Official Acceptance Mail.
  • Rejected. This means the paper will not be considered for the next process even if it is revised.

NOTE: Author should register to the conference only after obtaining the Official Acceptance Mail from the conference.

Plagiarism Policy for the Publication:

  • All articles submitted to the conference will first undergo a plagiarism check before being sent to our technical program committee for review.
  • Note that plagiarized articles will be rejected (Must be 20% below excluding references).
  • Copying of contents from other articles is strictly prohibited. However, it is allowed with proper quotations and citations.
  • Only articles with 80% original content should be submitted with the expectation of being accepted for IC-AISSTMH 2026 Book Chapter and Extended Version of Journal Publication.
  • IC-AISSTMH keenly discourages plagiarism in research articles, proposals and survey articles submitted to us.
  • Articles failing plagiarism check will be subjected to rejection without any review.

Instructions for the oral presentation:

The presentation should last about 15 minutes. You may use whatever type of presentation and number of slides you prefer. Please arrive early for your presentation to verify that all essential equipment is available.
