About the Conference

The International Multidisciplinary Conference on AI-Driven Sustainability in Science & Technology, Management, and Humanities (IC-AISSTMH 2026) aims to create a global platform for researchers, academicians, industry experts, and policymakers to deliberate on the transformative role of Artificial Intelligence in building sustainable futures. Organized by the School of Computational Sciences, PSG College of Arts & Science, the conference emphasizes interdisciplinary convergence, innovative AI models, and responsible technologies. The event highlights how AI-driven solutions can address complex societal, scientific, economic, and human-centric challenges while aligning with global sustainability goals and ethical frameworks.


Scope of the Conference

The conference scope spans theoretical foundations, applied research, and real-world implementations of AI for sustainability across multiple disciplines. It encourages cutting-edge research on intelligent systems, predictive analytics, automation, ethical AI, and decision-support frameworks. Contributions focusing on sustainable development, green technologies, digital transformation, social impact, and inclusive growth are strongly encouraged. By integrating science, management, humanities, and emerging technologies, the conference promotes holistic perspectives that bridge technical innovation with human values, policy design, and long-term societal well-being.


About PSG College of Arts & Science

Established in 1947, PSG College of Arts and Science (PSGCAS) has been a beacon of quality education in Coimbatore, nurturing generations of students with a passion for knowledge and holistic development. Founded under the aegis of the PSG & Sons Charities, the college embodies a vision of academic excellence, social responsibility, and ethical leadership. From its humble beginnings, PSGCAS has grown into a premier institution offering diverse programs in Arts, Science, Commerce, and Management, fostering critical thinking, creativity, and innovation. The college emphasizes a student-centered approach, combining rigorous academics with co-curricular and extracurricular opportunities, ensuring the development of well-rounded individuals prepared to meet the challenges of a rapidly evolving world. PSGCAS is committed to research, innovation, and industry collaboration, enabling students and faculty to engage in meaningful projects that address real-world challenges. The college has consistently evolved its curriculum to stay aligned with global standards, incorporating modern teaching methodologies, technology-driven learning, and experiential opportunities that enrich the educational journey.
Beyond academics, PSGCAS prides itself on a vibrant campus life that encourages cultural engagement, sports, and community service. Clubs, societies, and events provide students with platforms to explore their passions, develop leadership skills, and cultivate a sense of social responsibility. This holistic approach ensures that graduates emerge not only as skilled professionals but also as empathetic, socially conscious citizens. Over the decades, PSGCAS has forged strong alumni networks, many of whom have become leaders in diverse fields, reflecting the institution’s enduring impact on society. The college’s ethos of excellence, integrity, and inclusivity continues to guide its mission, making it a preferred destination for students seeking a transformative educational experience.
Today, PSG College of Arts and Science stands as a symbol of knowledge, innovation, and values, committed to empowering young minds, inspiring lifelong learning, and contributing positively to society. Its story is one of vision, perseverance, and a relentless pursuit of excellence, shaping the future one student at a time.


About School of Computational Sciences

The School of Computational Sciences at PSG College of Arts & Science (Autonomous) offers a comprehensive portfolio of two postgraduate and six undergraduate programmes, including MCA, M.Sc. Software Systems, B.Sc. Computer Science, B.Sc. Information Technology, B.Sc. Computer Technology, B.Sc. Computer Science with Data Analytics, B.Sc. Networking and Mobile Applications, and BCA. The School is supported by a team of highly qualified and experienced faculty members who bring together academic excellence, industry exposure, and research expertise to deliver an outcome-based, industry-driven curriculum. The School is equipped with state-of-the-art computing infrastructure, advanced laboratories, and licensed software platforms that enable experiential and practical learning. A strong emphasis is placed on research, innovation, and technology-driven problem solving, encouraging students to explore emerging domains such as Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Cybersecurity, IoT, Cloud Computing, and Advanced Networking. Research and Development forms the cornerstone of the School, with students actively trained to innovate, publish, and translate ideas into impactful solutions.
This international conference serves as a vital platform that connects faculty members, research scholars, and students, fostering knowledge exchange, interdisciplinary collaboration, and exposure to global research perspectives in cutting-edge computational and AI-driven technologies.


Who can attend ?

The International Multidisciplinary Conference on AI-Driven Sustainability in Science & Technology, Management, and Humanities is open to a wide spectrum of participants, including:
  • Academicians and Faculty Members from universities, colleges, and research institutions
  • Researchers and Research Scholars (Ph.D./Post-Doctoral Fellows) working in interdisciplinary and AI-enabled domains
  • Industry Professionals and Practitioners from IT, Analytics, AI, IoT, Healthcare, Finance, and Emerging Technologies
  • Undergraduate and Postgraduate Students from Science, Technology, Management, and Humanities streams
  • Entrepreneurs, Start-ups, and Innovation Leaders working on sustainable and AI-driven solutions
  • Policy Makers, Think Tanks, and Social Innovators involved in digital transformation and sustainability initiatives
The conference encourages interdisciplinary participation, fostering collaboration, knowledge exchange, and innovation across academic, industrial, and societal ecosystems.
