About the Conference

The International Multidisciplinary Conference on AI-Driven Sustainability in Science & Technology, Management, and Humanities provides a global platform for researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and students to explore the transformative role of Artificial Intelligence in achieving sustainable development.
The conference emphasizes an interdisciplinary approach, integrating technological innovation with managerial practices and human-centered perspectives. It focuses on how AI can address complex global challenges while ensuring ethical responsibility, social impact, and long-term sustainability.
By fostering knowledge exchange and cross-disciplinary collaboration, the conference aims to promote responsible AI innovation , encourage impactful research, and contribute to sustainable solutions that benefit society, industry, and the global community.


About the College

Established in 1947, PSG College of Arts and Science (PSGCAS) has been a beacon of quality education in Coimbatore, nurturing generations of students with a passion for knowledge and holistic development. Founded under the aegis of the PSG & Sons Charities, the college embodies a vision of academic excellence, social responsibility, and ethical leadership.
PSGCAS is committed to research, innovation, and industry collaboration, enabling students and faculty to engage in meaningful projects that address real-world challenges. The college has consistently evolved its curriculum to stay aligned with global standards, incorporating modern teaching methodologies, technology-driven learning, and experiential opportunities that enrich the educational journey.
PSG College of Arts and Science stands as a symbol of knowledge, innovation, and values, committed to empowering young minds, inspiring lifelong learning, and contributing positively to society. Its story is one of vision, perseverance, and a relentless pursuit of excellence, shaping the future one student at a time.

Call For Papers

Original Articles, Research Papers, Case Studies that highlight the issues related to the theme can be submitted for presentation in the conference. The papers submitted for the conference should be unpublished and original works in the following areas:
AI in Science & Technology
  • AI for environmental sustainability and climate modeling
  • Smart technologies in engineering and infrastructure
  • Data science and computational innovation
AI in Management & Business
  • AI in strategic decision-making
  • Sustainable finance and economics
  • Human resource management and organizational behavior
AI in Humanities & Social Sciences
  • Societal and ethical impacts of AI
  • AI and cultural studies
  • Language, communication, and digital media influences
